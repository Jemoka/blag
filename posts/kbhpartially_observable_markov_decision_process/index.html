Partially Observable Markov Decision Process is a Markov Decision Process with State Uncertainty.

As always we desire to find a policy \(\pi\) such that we can:

\begin{equation} \underset{\pi \in \Pi}{\text{maximize}}\ \mathbb{E} \qty[ \sum_{t=0}^{\infty} \gamma^{t} R(b_{t}, \pi(b_{t}))] \end{equation}

whereby our policy \(\pi\) instead of taking in a state for input takes in a belief (probability distribution over possible states) as input.
