Prototyping

Last edited: September 9, 2022 the fast you are willing to prototype, the more willing you are to fail, the faster you will get to a successful partial solution you can refine and repeat. how to prototype faster? In order of decreasing slowness—- build out the whole product… building the minimum viable product… skeleton prototyping (Figma)… Pen and paper… Talking about it The trade-off: each level gives increased fidelity: its closer to what will actually ship, so you can get better+detailed feedback.
