Capital-Asset Pricing Model

Last edited: October 10, 2022 CAPM is a method of portfolio selection analysis which focuses on maximizing return given some fixed variance. It deals with optimal Capital Market Line, given here: \begin{equation} E[R_{p}] = r_{f}+\frac{\sigma_{p}}{\sigma_{T}}\qty(E[R_{T}]-r_{f}) \end{equation} Which describes \(E[R_{p}]\), the expected return of an optimal portfolio in a market, given, \(R_{T}\) is the market return, \(r_{f}\) is the risk-free rate, \(\sigma_{p}\) is the portfolio returns, and \(\sigma_{t}\) is standard-deviation of the market returns.
