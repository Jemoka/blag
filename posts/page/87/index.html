Last edited: May 5, 2022 constructor theory deals with “constructors”, a general type of computer. constructor theory can give us a theory of the universal quantum constructor by expanding upon quantum information theory. It allows us to unify quantum and classical information by simply defining operations in terms of counterfactuals exclusively: that a space is entirely defined by what’s possible and what’s not possible. According to constructor theory, fundamental laws are not dynamical laws instead are boundary conditions.
