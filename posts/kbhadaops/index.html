KLD Sampling

KLD Sampling uses KL Divergence to approximate difference between two probability distributions:

\begin{equation} N \approx \frac{k-1}{2\xi} \qty(1- \frac{2}{9(k-1)} + \sqrt{\frac{2}{9(k-1)}} z_{1-\eta})^{3} \end{equation}
