Scratch

Let \(L \in P\), and \(M\) be the polytime TM that decides \(L\). Define \(M’\) to be \(M\) with \(q_{\text{accept}}\) and \(q_{\text{reject}}\) swapped. Fact: \(M’\) decides \(\neg L\).
