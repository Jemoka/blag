Capacitor discharging:

\begin{equation}
Q = Q\_0 e^{-\frac{t}{RC}}
\end{equation}

where \\(Q\\) is capacitor change at time \\(t\\), and \\(Q\_0\\) initial change, and \\(RC\\) the [resistance]({{< relref "KBhcurrent.md#resistance-of-a-wire" >}}) and [capacitance]({{< relref "KBhcapacitance.md" >}}).
