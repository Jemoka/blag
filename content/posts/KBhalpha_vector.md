\begin{equation}
U^{\pi}(b) = \sum\_{s}^{} b(s) U^{\pi}(s) = {\alpha\_{\pi}}^{\top} b
\end{equation}

where \\(\alpha\_{\pi}\\) is the [utility of the policy]({{< relref "KBhpolicy_evaluation.md" >}}) at each actual state.

\begin{equation}
\alpha\_{\pi} = \qty[ U^{\pi}(s\_1), U^{\pi}(s\_2) ]
\end{equation}
